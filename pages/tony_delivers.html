Tony Delivers: the cult classic, one-man DoorDash killer. I helped my friend Tony create the *branding*, *marketing* and *website* for his new food delivery service.

Seattle's minimum wage ordinance for gig workers was causing chaos in the market; Tony was on the verge of quitting UberEats. Orders were drying up and people were pissed at the higher fees. We got together and talked it out. In the next few nights, I put a poster, logo and website together.

{% include image_card.html alt="tony delivers tweet and advertising" src="tony-delivers.jpg" %}

Yes, it's a little rough around the edges, but that might just be what makes it work. Flash and polish are just one tool in the bag. I try not to make things more complicated than they need to be.
